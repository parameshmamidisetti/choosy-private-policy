# Child Safety Policy – Choosy

Choosy, developed and maintained by Paramesh Mamidisetti, is committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

## 1. Zero Tolerance for CSAE & CSAM

Choosy strictly prohibits any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). This includes:

* Creation, distribution, or possession of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
* Grooming or exploitation of minors
* Any inappropriate interaction involving minors

Any violation will result in immediate account suspension, permanent banning, and reporting to appropriate authorities.

## 2. Reporting & Enforcement

Choosy actively monitors and responds to reports of abuse. Users can report any suspicious or harmful activity directly within the app. All reports are reviewed promptly, and necessary action is taken.

## 3. In-App Safety Features

To ensure user safety, Choosy provides:

* Report user/content functionality
* Block user feature to prevent unwanted interactions

## 4. Child Safety Contact

If you have concerns related to child safety, please contact us:

Email: choosyapp@outlook.com

## 5. Compliance with Laws

Choosy complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations and cooperates fully with law enforcement agencies when required.
